Vienna Boys' Choir

Date:11/3/2024

Time: 6:00pm

Location:The Sail Melaka

The Vienna Boys' Choir, established in 1498, is one of the world's oldest choirs. Renowned for their exceptional musical talent and captivating voices, this Austrian choir presents a timeless and unique musical tradition. An unmissable journey into music!


Choir Highlights:


  • Exceptional Musical Talent: Experience the extraordinary musical talent of the Vienna Boys' Choir.
  • Captivating Voices: Be enchanted by the captivating voices of the choir members.
  • Timeless Tradition: Immerse yourself in a timeless musical tradition dating back to 1498.
  • Unique Musical Journey: Embark on an unmissable journey into the world of music with the Vienna Boys' Choir.
